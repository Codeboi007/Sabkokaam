About SabKoKaam

Empowering rural communities through meaningful employment and economic opportunities since 2023

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To bridge the employment gap in rural India by connecting skilled and unskilled workers with reliable job opportunities through accessible technology.

Our Vision

A future where every rural Indian has access to stable employment, fair wages, and the opportunity to improve their quality of life without leaving their communities.

Our Values

Accessibility, Transparency, Fairness, Rural Empowerment, and Community Development are the core principles that guide everything we do.

Our Story

SabKoKaam was founded in 2023 by a group of technologists and social entrepreneurs who witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by rural communities in finding stable employment.

After seeing how many skilled workers were forced to migrate to crowded cities, leaving their families behind, we decided to create a solution that would bring opportunities directly to rural doorsteps.

What began as a small pilot project in three villages has now grown into a platform that serves thousands of workers across multiple states in India. Our voice-enabled, multilingual platform ensures that even those with limited literacy can access dignified work.

Today, SabKoKaam is not just an employment platform but a movement to transform rural livelihoods and reduce distress migration by creating sustainable local economies.
