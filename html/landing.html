Connecting Rural India with Employment Opportunities

SabKoKaam helps unskilled workers in rural areas find reliable jobs and secure their livelihoods through a simple, accessible platform.

Find Work Hire Workers

Why Choose SabKoKaam?

Our platform is designed specifically for rural areas, making job hunting simple for everyone.

Local Opportunities

Find work close to your village without migrating to cities. Connect with employers in your area for construction, agriculture, and service jobs.

Voice Support

Our platform supports voice commands and notifications in multiple regional languages, making it accessible even if you can't read.

Secure Payments

Get paid fairly and on time through our secure payment system. No more unpaid work or payment delays.

How SabKoKaam Works

Finding work should be simple. Our process makes it easy for everyone.

1

Register

Create a simple profile with your skills, location, and availability. No complex forms or documents needed.

2

Find Jobs

Browse local job opportunities or receive voice alerts when new jobs match your skills in your area.

3

Get Hired

Connect with employers, agree on terms, and start working with the security of our platform.

4

Get Paid

Receive payment directly to your account or through local payment methods you trust.
