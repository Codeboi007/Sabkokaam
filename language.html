How SabKoKaam Works

Finding work should be simple. Our process makes it easy for everyone.

1

Register

Create a simple profile with your skills, location, and availability. No complex forms or documents needed.

2

Find Jobs

Browse local job opportunities or receive voice alerts when new jobs match your skills in your area.

3

Get Hired

Connect with employers, agree on terms, and start working with the security of our platform.

4

Get Paid

Receive payment directly to your account or through local payment methods you trust.
