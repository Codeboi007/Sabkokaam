Worker Safety Tips

Before Accepting a Job

Always verify the employer's identity through SabKoKaam's verification system.

Check employer ratings and reviews from other workers before accepting work.

Get clear details about the job, including location, duration, and payment terms in writing.

At the Workplace

Always use proper safety equipment provided for your job type.

If you feel unsafe at any worksite, you have the right to leave and report through our app.

Keep the SabKoKaam emergency helpline number (1800-XXX-XXXX) saved on your phone.

Payment Protection

Never accept direct cash payments outside the SabKoKaam platform to ensure payment protection.

Report any payment delays immediately through the app for quick resolution.

Keep track of your working hours and agreed rate through the app's work diary feature.

Personal Information Safety

Never share your bank account details or Aadhaar number directly with employers.

Use the SabKoKaam secure messaging system for all job-related communications.

Report any suspicious requests for personal information to our support team immediately.

Employer Guidelines

⚠️ AGE REQUIREMENT: All workers must be 18+ years of age. Hiring minors is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension.

Legal Requirements

Register your business with appropriate local authorities before posting jobs.

Comply with all labor laws including minimum wage requirements in your state/region.

Provide proper work agreements/contracts for jobs lasting more than 7 days.

Worker Safety

Provide all necessary safety equipment for the job as per industry standards.

Ensure proper training is given before workers start hazardous tasks.

Maintain clean, safe work environments with access to drinking water and restroom facilities.

Payment Guidelines

All payments must be processed through the SabKoKaam platform.

Release payment promptly upon completion of agreed work.

Failure to pay workers will result in penalties and possible account termination.

Job Posting Guidelines

Clearly specify job requirements, duration, location, and compensation.

No discriminatory hiring practices based on gender, caste, religion, or other protected characteristics.

No misleading job descriptions or bait-and-switch practices.

Important Notice:

Employers must maintain a minimum 3-star rating to continue using the platform. Repeated violations of guidelines may result in permanent deactivation of your account. SabKoKaam reserves the right to remove job postings that violate our community standards.

Prohibited Activities

No illegal activities or jobs (including alcohol/tobacco sales to minors).

No requesting personal favors or activities outside the scope of professional work.

No offering housing accommodations in exchange for work without proper compensation.

No requiring workers to pay fees to secure employment.
